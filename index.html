Management Liability Insurance

SD Insurance's Management Liability Insurance is essential for businesses that want to protect their management team from potential legal liabilities. This insurance provides coverage for directors, officers, and managers against claims arising from wrongful acts, employment practices, and other management-related risks. Whether you run a small business or a large corporation, our Management Liability Insurance offers the peace of mind you need to focus on growing your business.

Key Features of Management Liability Insurance

  • Directors and Officers Liability: Protection for directors and officers against claims arising from wrongful acts committed in their capacity as company leaders.
  • Employment Practices Liability: Coverage for claims related to employment practices, such as wrongful termination, discrimination, and harassment.
  • Statutory Liability: Protection against fines and penalties imposed by regulatory authorities for breaches of legislation.
  • Crime Coverage: Coverage for financial losses resulting from fraudulent or dishonest acts committed by employees.
  • Tax Audit Coverage: Protection against the costs associated with tax audits and investigations.
  • 24/7 Claims Support: Access to a dedicated claims team available around the clock to assist you in the event of an incident.
